Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Overview

iRetroMusic ("the App") is developed by John Seong. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

iRetroMusic does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The App operates entirely on your device.

Apple Music Access

The App requests access to your Apple Music library through Apple's MusicKit framework. This access is used solely to:

  • Display your song and album library within the retro player interface
  • Play music from your library through the built-in player

Your music library data never leaves your device. The App does not upload, cache, or transmit any information about your music library to any server.

Third-Party Services

iRetroMusic does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs that collect data in the background.

Local Storage

The App may store minimal preferences (such as volume level or backlight settings) locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data is not accessible to any external service.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. The App contains no user accounts, no social features, and no data collection of any kind.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please visit our support page or open an issue on GitHub.
