Support

Frequently asked questions and how to get help.

How do I use the click wheel?

Drag your finger in a circular motion on the wheel ring to scroll through menus. Tap the center button to select. Tap MENU (top) to go back, and use the left/right areas for previous/next track.

Why is my music library empty?

iRetroMusic requires Apple Music access to display your library. Go to Settings > iRetroMusic and ensure music access is enabled. You need songs in your Apple Music library (purchased or from an Apple Music subscription).

Does it work without Apple Music?

The app will launch and you can explore the menus and play the Vortex game, but the music library will be empty without Apple Music access.

How do I play the Vortex game?

Navigate to Extras > Games > Vortex. Rotate the click wheel to move the paddle left and right. Press the center button to launch the ball. Press MENU to exit back to the menu.

Why does the iPad look different?

On iPad, iRetroMusic shows a classic desktop-style music manager with sidebar navigation, column browser, and search. The click wheel interface is exclusive to iPhone.

Does the app collect my data?

No. iRetroMusic does not collect, store, or transmit any data. Your music library is accessed locally and never leaves your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.
